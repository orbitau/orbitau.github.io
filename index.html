Orbitau - Seven Deadly Sins, a fantasy game inspired by Norse Mythology and the Seven Deadly Sins, auspiciously provides players with new experiences based on well-known legends.

In Orbitau, players will directly participate in the Demon - God war with the familiar role-playing and turn-based strategy gameplay in a magical land of mixed reality and fiction created by blockchain technology. And the rewards are materially valuable, not just symbolic victories.
The maps are built based on different elements, representing each land in hell,
and containing the dangerous lord of that land.
We believe that games are for entertainment purposes, so we have painstakingly designed the plot, gameplay, graphics, and music - since we highly value your personal journeys.
DAILY QUEST
Everyday the task list will be renewed and you will always receive a reward for your hard work.

The daily quest also reveals a part of the plot.
WEEKLY QUEST
In addition to the daily and weekly missions, players will also be faced with greater challenges, and of course, receive greater rewards.
TOWER
According to the story, players will go through towers of increasing complexity according to their current power level, and receive many high-value rewards along the journey to conquer the lands.
GUILD
Once you become a member of a Clan/Guild, you will join other players in Clan development activities such as building clan facilities, boss hunting, conquering territory, and profit from taxing.
HERO
Players begin their journey with three heroes, each with his/her own skill set: you can freely upgrade and arrange your own gods to devise the most effective strategy.
INVENTORY
Using their inventory, players can combine accessories, design squads, power up heroes, and build their own unique assets.
SHOP
The shop is a place for players to wander around the magnificent treasures, to relieve stress, and to know what value they will receive for their efforts in Orbitau.
TERRITORY SYSTEM
With four levels of "Common," "Rare," "Legend," and "Epic" and a set of five elements "Ember," "Ocean," "Earth," "Forest," and "Ore," players can create a plethora of different strategies based on their own preferences.
Most importantly, you can take ownership over nearly everything in the game and reap real benefits of your own accomplishments.
